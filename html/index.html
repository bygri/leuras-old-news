The National Library of Australia, with the assistance of the Blue Mountains City Library, the State Library of NSW and Springwood Historians, has recently released digitised versions of newspapers from as early as 1889 using the NLA's Trove service.

As a Leura resident, I am interested in the early history of the township and decided to read my way through every reference to Leura I could find. I figured I might as well transcribe and annotate as many of these articles along the way as I can so other people might have an easier time of it in future. I hope you find this page useful.

I have a Facebook Page for this site where I will occasionally post interesting tidbits I come across. Follow me there to keep up-to-date.
