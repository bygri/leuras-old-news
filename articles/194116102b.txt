Precis: Robert Marshall fought a kangaroo in Leura (and his pants were ripped open).

#marshall-robert
----
Robert Marshall had a terrible encounter with an old man kangaroo on the higher
ground of Leura estate, and his pants were ripped open. He killed the kangaroo
with a bowie knife, and sent the tail down to the Centennial Hotel to make soup
for the miners, who like the kangaroo dished up.
