Precis: Notice of meeting to inaugurate the Jockey Club.

#racecourse
----
Meeting to-night at 8, in Mr. Nimmo's Hotel, to inaugurate the Katoomba and
Leura Jockey Club, and to agitate to have a good road made to the Racecourse
Oval on Leura Estate.
